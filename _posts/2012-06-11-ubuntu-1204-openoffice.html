--- layout: post title: 換裝 OpenOffice 3.4 (Ubuntu 12.04) date: '2012-06-11T15:11:00.000+08:00' author: Chih-En Wu tags: modified_time: '2013-03-16T01:56:19.980+08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-6518933078301809282.post-7908254567021365422 blogger_orig_url: http://trunone.blogspot.com/2012/06/ubuntu-1204-openoffice.html --- 下載 OpenOffice 3.4: http://www.openoffice.org/download/index.html

移除 LibreOffice :
$ sudo apt-get purge libreoffice*

解壓縮 OpenOffice 3.4:
$ tar zxvf FILENAME

安裝 OpenOffice 3.4:
$ cd INSTALL_FOLDER/DEBS
$ sudo dpkg -i *.deb
$ cd desktop-integration
$ sudo dpkg -i *.deb
