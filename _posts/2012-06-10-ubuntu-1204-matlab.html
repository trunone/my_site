--- layout: post title: 啟動 MATLAB 出現錯誤訊息 (Ubuntu 12.04) date: '2012-06-10T22:53:00.000+08:00' author: Chih-En Wu tags: - Ubuntu - MATLAB modified_time: '2013-03-16T01:55:44.593+08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-6518933078301809282.post-6914195156492765571 blogger_orig_url: http://trunone.blogspot.com/2012/06/ubuntu-1204-matlab.html --- 若啟動 MATLAB 有出現下列錯誤訊息時
/user/local/bin/matlab: 1: /usr/local/MATLAB/R2010b/bin/util/oscheck.sh: /lib64/libc.so.6: not found

請服用下列指令

64-bit OS:
$ sudo ln -s /lib/x86_64-linux-gnu/libc-2.15.so /lib64/libc.so.6

32-bit OS:
$ sudo ln -s /lib/i386-linux-gnu/libc-2.15.so /lib/libc.so.6
