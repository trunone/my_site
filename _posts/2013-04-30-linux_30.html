--- layout: post title: 設定 Linux 函式庫路徑 date: '2013-04-30T09:15:00.000+08:00' author: Chih-En Wu tags: - Linux - Ubuntu modified_time: '2013-05-04T14:07:17.956+08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-6518933078301809282.post-1693523540080201346 blogger_orig_url: http://trunone.blogspot.com/2013/04/linux_30.html --- 在 Linux 下增加程式讀取函式庫路徑的方法

當執行時：
% LD_LIBRARY_PATH=/path/to/custom/lib Command

新增到環境變數：
% export LD_LIBRARY_PATH=/path/to/custom/lib

新增到系統設定中：
% sudo ldconfig /path/to/custom/lib/
